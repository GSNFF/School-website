Intro to PMBHSS

Paramount Boarding Higher Secondary School (PMBHSS) is one of the finest and the reputed school of the country, situated inWestern Nepal . It is one of the nation’s leading School Chains with branches spread across the country. PMBHSS is a qualified school, where children run to school not because they ‘have’ to, but because they 'want' to

The education system over here in PMBHSS is unique in the sense of progression and succession which aims at making learning interesting by making it more practical oriented. In addition, the focus at PMBHSS is on inculcating skills such as public speaking, making presentations, personal grooming, team work, etc. with special emphasis on the application of knowledge to everyday situations.

PMBHSS is well equipped in all sorts of physical infrastructure and facilities, well modern buildings comfortable and well furnished classrooms, fully equipped science lab, library, hostel facilities with attached washrooms and bath rooms, sufficient water supply, big playground, bus services etc. are some of the important features of this school. The school runs from Montessori to standard ten and also +2 level(HSEB) the number of students strength is above 2500.
